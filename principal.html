Message from the Principal

Education is not merely the acquisition of knowledge; it is the formation of character, values, and responsible citizenship. At Carmel Convent School, we strive to create a nurturing and disciplined environment where every child is encouraged to grow intellectually, morally, and socially.

Our aim is to provide quality education rooted in academic excellence, Christian values, and a spirit of service. We believe that each child is unique and possesses immense potential, which can be nurtured through proper guidance, encouragement, and dedication.

I look forward to the cooperation of parents, teachers, and students in making Carmel Convent School a center of learning where wisdom and excellence walk hand in hand.

Sr. Jancy
