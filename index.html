Wisdom and Excellence

Carmel Convent School, Manasa is a Catholic Christian Minority Institution committed to the holistic development of children through academic excellence, moral values, and disciplined learning.

Welcome to Carmel Convent School

Established in 2003 and managed by the Sisters of the Congregation of the Mother of Carmel (CMC), Carmel Convent School, Manasa provides quality education at an affordable cost. The school follows the CBSE curriculum and offers education from Nursery to Class XII in an English medium environment.

Academic Excellence

A strong CBSE-based curriculum supported by experienced teachers, modern teaching methods, and continuous assessment.

Value-Based Education

Education rooted in Christian principles, promoting discipline, compassion, honesty, and responsibility.

Holistic Development

Equal importance to academics, sports, cultural activities, and character formation.
