About the School

Carmel Convent School, Manasa is a Catholic Christian Minority Institution established in 2003. The school is managed by the Sisters of the Congregation of the Mother of Carmel (CMC) and is committed to providing quality education rooted in moral values, discipline, and academic excellence.

Vision

To develop responsible human personalities with strong moral values, academic competence, and a sense of social responsibility, guided by faith and integrity.

Mission

  • To provide holistic education that nurtures mind, body, and spirit.
  • To promote academic excellence through disciplined learning.
  • To instill values of honesty, compassion, and respect.
  • To prepare students to meet the challenges of a changing world.

School Motto

“Wisdom and Excellence”
