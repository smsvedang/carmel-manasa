Academic Programme

Carmel Convent School, Manasa follows the curriculum prescribed by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). The academic programme is designed to promote conceptual clarity, critical thinking, and all-round development.

Classes Offered

  • Pre-Primary: Nursery, LKG, UKG
  • Primary: Classes I – V
  • Middle School: Classes VI – VIII
  • Secondary: Classes IX – X
  • Senior Secondary: Classes XI – XII

Medium of Instruction

English is the medium of instruction for all classes. Emphasis is laid on effective communication skills, reading habits, and language proficiency.

Assessment & Evaluation

  • Continuous assessment as per CBSE guidelines
  • Periodic tests, assignments, and projects
  • Terminal examinations
  • Regular feedback to parents

Co-Curricular Activities

The school encourages participation in sports, cultural programmes, debates, competitions, and value-based activities to ensure holistic development of students.
