Management

Carmel Convent School, Manasa is managed by the Sisters of the Congregation of the Mother of Carmel (CMC), a religious congregation dedicated to education and social service. The management ensures that the institution functions in accordance with CBSE norms and the values of Christian education.

Principal’s Message

Education at Carmel Convent School is a journey towards knowledge, character formation, and responsible citizenship. We aim to create a nurturing environment where students are encouraged to excel academically while growing into disciplined and compassionate individuals.

Administrative Commitment

  • Transparent and fair academic practices
  • Qualified and dedicated teaching staff
  • Safe and disciplined school environment
  • Regular communication with parents
  • Strict adherence to CBSE guidelines
