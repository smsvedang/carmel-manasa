Admissions

Carmel Convent School, Manasa follows a transparent and fair admission process, in accordance with CBSE norms and the values of the institution.

Admission Procedure

  • Admission is granted subject to availability of seats.
  • Registration forms are available at the school office.
  • Admissions are based on interaction / assessment as applicable.
  • Age criteria is followed as per CBSE and state guidelines.
  • Submission of required documents is mandatory.

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate (issued by competent authority)
  • Transfer Certificate (for Class II onwards)
  • Previous School Report Card
  • Aadhaar Card (Student & Parents)
  • Passport size photographs

Rules & Regulations

  • The school uniform is compulsory for all students.
  • Regular attendance and punctuality are mandatory.
  • Students are expected to maintain discipline and respect.
  • Use of mobile phones is strictly prohibited on campus.
  • Parents are requested to cooperate with school policies.
  • The school reserves the right to amend rules when necessary.
